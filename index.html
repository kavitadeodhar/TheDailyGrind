Overview.

Social Media is a daily diary for many people. It is now easy to let your voice be heard even if it is about a simple thing like our regular cup of coffee. There are many options and people tend to be loyal to their brands. The intent is to explore which brand do people favor based on their tweets. We will also try to see if a region favors one brand over the other. Also the intent is to explore whether momentary emotion conveyed via a tweet has any correlation on customer satisfaction index. We will compare CSAT data from ACSI(American customer satisfaction index) to the scores generated using our code.

Objectives

Here’s what would like to learn and accomplish:

  • Perform sentiment analysis on twitter data for various coffee shop brands and determine which brand is most favored.
  • Does brand preference change in different parts of the country?
  • Perform regression analysis and determine if emotion expressed on twitter ties into/affects/compares to customer satisfaction index calculated using multivariate customer experience data.
  • We would like to see if there is a trend (plot against time) but we may not be able to do it due to the size of data.

Approach

Scrap the data From where and how are you collecting your data? 1.Tweets with hash tags of various coffee brands will be our source of data. We will be using the twitteR package. 2.ACSI data from ACSI website for Quick Serve Restaurants like Dunkin, Starbucks, Mc Donalds etc. 3.Dataset (text, csv) containing positive and negative words.
